Würzburg.digital is a 100% open community to connect people from within the digital ecosystem around Würzburg. So if you're a startup, an entrepreneur, working in the digital space or just want to meet open-minded people - Feel free to join us on the Würzburg.digital #Slack-Channel.

Community first

We know how hard it is to stay connected while you are changing the world. So that is why we created a digital space where you can make meaningful connections, inspire and learn from another, share your ideas, get feedback and support. We believe that together we can build an digital ecosystem and actively design our tomorrow.

See some of our topics and channels

Topics

Our topics are based on mainly three believes. Digitalization will change every part of our lives. The ability to innovate continuously is the only reason why organizations will survive or die. It's all about the people - only together can we shape the future.
